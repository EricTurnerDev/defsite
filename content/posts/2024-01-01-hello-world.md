## Why Clojure?

Clojure rewards you for thinking carefully about data and transformations. The
language has a small surface area, persistent immutable data structures by
default, and a REPL that makes exploratory programming feel natural.

```clojure
;; The whole point, in one expression
(->> data
     (filter interesting?)
     (map transform)
     (reduce combine))
```

Once you internalize the idea that a program is mostly a pipeline of
transformations over plain data, a lot of complexity melts away.
